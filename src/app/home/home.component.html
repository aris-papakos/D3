Daily Urban Crime System
Personalizable map of criminal incidents in Greater London

A Daily Urban System refers to an area around a city, in which daily commuting occurs. It does not just describe the city itself, but includes all the area's from which individual commute. By combining all movement it is possible to define an urban region, which extends to outside the central city. The application focusses on the London Daily urban System. The urban reagion of London is generally described as Greater London. According to the 2011 census the Greater London area has a population of 8.173.941. The City of London itself is actually a much smaller area with less than 10.000 residents.

While a Daily Urban System takes all movement to all area's within it is created based on individual movements. Movements include the commute from your home to work, but could also be the bus ride from to your favourite pub when your finish you workday. You are able to create your own individual Daily Urban System by selecting various locations which you visit frequently, the neighbourhood (Ward) you live in for example. Your Daily Urban System will serve as input for mapping crime report in the Greater London area and give you some insights in how safe your Dailt Urban System really is.
